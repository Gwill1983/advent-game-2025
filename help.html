Help & Instructions

Welcome to The Advent Game — your festive mission to help Santa solve a Christmas mystery.

Objective

Your goal is to uncover three critical details before Christmas Eve:

  • Who sent the mysterious letter
  • What gift they received
  • Where they are in the world

Daily Challenges

Each day in December, a new challenge will unlock on the calendar. Solve the riddle to find a what3words location, then visit it in the real world to uncover a QR code or hidden message that reveals a clue.

Every correct discovery will update your Case File, helping you eliminate incorrect suspects, gifts, and locations.

Tips for Success

  • Check the calendar daily — new clues unlock automatically each morning.
  • Keep your phone charged and location services enabled for on-the-go puzzles.
  • Collaborate with your family or teammates — every clue matters!

Need More Help?

If you get stuck, you can revisit previous days or use small hints hidden around the game interface. But remember — Santa’s watching, so use them wisely!
